NETTREK ULTRA · Help

Goal

Survive as long as you can. When your structural health (hull) hits 0, the game ends. Score = kills + planets captured + seconds survived.

Universe

300,000 × 300,000 unit galaxy with 40 planets in 4 sectors. You and three AI fleets compete; friendly ships at your home help defend you. Capture enemy / neutral planets to grow your team's permanent bonuses (max hull, shield, energy, repair).

Structural Health (Hull)

  • <50% — torpedoes offline
  • <25% — phasers offline
  • <10% — warp drive offline
  • 0% — destroyed (game over)

Shields & Energy

Shields up drain energy but absorb all damage. Hit 0 shield HP → shields collapse, can't be raised for 2.5s; bleed-through damage to hull. Phasers cost 5% of max energy per shot. Torps cost a flat amount plus consume from a limited magazine (reload ~8–12s depending on class). One single energy pool powers warp, shields, phasers, torps.

Capturing a planet

Orbit an enemy/neutral planet (press O while close + slow). Press C and hold for 5s with no enemy within range. Captured planets grant permanent stat bonuses to your team and provide repair/fuel/refit stops. Orbiting your home instantly tops up everything.

Combat ranges

  • Phaser cone: ±30° (wider, close range only)
  • Torpedo cone: ±20° (narrower, can fly to 15,000u with falling hit chance)
  • Torp hit probability: 100% at ~3,000u → 20% at 15,000u
  • Torp speed: launches slow, accelerates to warp 5 (750 u/s)

Radar IFF

  • LONG (60,000u): all planets show green, all ships show as uniform pale contacts. You can't tell friend from foe.
  • SHORT (15,000u): planets in team color, friendlies in your team color, enemies in red.
  • Auto-Lock+Nav requires confirmed enemy = short-radar range.

CADET BRIEFING

Welcome aboard, Cadet. Your ship is ready and your auto-systems are already on. Here's how to play.

How to play

  • Press L to attack. Your ship will pick the nearest enemy, fly to them, and shoot automatically.
  • Press H when you're low on ammo or energy. Your ship flies home. Orbit your home planet for 5 seconds and everything refills.
  • Feeling brave? Click an enemy planet on the radar and press C to capture it. Captured planets make your team stronger.
  • Auto-Defend has your back. When enemies fire torpedoes at you, your ship deploys flares to knock them down. If it runs out of flares, it flies away until you're safe.

Reading the screen

Tactical view (the big black canvas): close-up combat space, ~3,000 units across at 1× zoom. Press +/− to zoom out up to 8×. This is where you fly, shoot, and orbit.

Radar widget (right side): your overview of the galaxy. Two modes you toggle with R:

  • SHORT (15,000u across) — planets in team color; friendlies in your color; enemies in red. Use this to pick targets.
  • LONG (60,000u across) — galactic view. All planets show green, all ships show as identical pale dots. You can see where things are but not who they are.

Auto-Lock+Nav only fires up when it has a confirmed enemy = something inside short-radar range.

Your home base

You start in orbit around your home planet. It's the only friendly planet you start with, and it's your safe spot.

  • Refill everything in 5 seconds. Press H any time to fly home. Orbit it and your energy, shields, ammo, and flares all refill.
  • It shoots back. Home has a phaser turret that fires at enemies that get close. If they destroy it, it rebuilds over 30 seconds.
  • Friendly ships patrol there. Two AI ships defend your home.
  • Call for help. If you're near home and in trouble, press Q and friendly ships will come rescue you.

Cadet bonuses

You get a much tougher ship in Cadet mode — 10× more shields, 3× more ammo, 3× more flares, and Auto-Defend on from the start. Use it to experiment.
